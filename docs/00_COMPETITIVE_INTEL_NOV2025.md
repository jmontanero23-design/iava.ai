- Voice engagement for balance, payments
- Fast access to fintech functions
- Growing rapidly in 2025

- Clean interfaces, no distractions
- Main features highlighted
- Subtle animations

- Expected by default in trading apps

- Premium, futuristic feel

- Reduces friction

- Minimal data entry

- Savings challenges, rewards
- Achievement systems
- Makes finance less stressful
- **Example:** Revolut - significant increase in DAU
